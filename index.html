It's All About me

A Brief History

  • 1998-2009

    Career Beginnings

    I began working in the residential construction industry. I quickly worked my way up to a project manager, overseeing $1M plus annually in reciepts. I helped design, create, and install various projects around Colorado. I worked at tradeshows and assisted in marketing.

  • 2009-2014

    Manufacture Representative and Business Owner

    In 2009 I decided to start my own business, and utilizing the contacts I made from my previous years as a residential contractor, I was asked to be a manufacturing representative for a start-up company. At the same time, I started my own business locally. As a manufacturing representative, I traveled across the United States, meeting with clients, setting up distributors, and working trade shows.

  • 2013-2018

    A New Challenge

    In 2013 I saw a nitch in the construction industry and began the new challenge of designing, creating, and manufacturing custom cabinetry and furniture for high-end vacation homes.

  • May 2018

    Bootcamp!

    I have always had great interest in the tech industry, even taking classes online for a period of time, but I became quickly aware that being self-taught was not going to be enough. Never being one to back down from an intellectual challenge, I applied for the Full Stack Bootcamp (part-time program). I am currently working full-time while completing my bootcamp, gratudation in November 2018.

Bamazon

Welcome to Bamazon! This is an Amazon-like storefront.

Within the customer portal, use the prompts to view the inventory, choose an item you would like to purchase, along with the total quantity desired. If the store has adequate inventory you will be able to complete the purchase and the stock number will be updated accordingly. However, if we do not have enough of the selected item in stock deeming us unable to fulfill your purchase, the transaction will not go through and you will be prompted to lower you desired quantity.
